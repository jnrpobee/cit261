The Lord Speaks to us

In the 9th verse of the article of faith of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints states that "we believe all that God has reveived all that He does now reveal, and we believe that He will yet reveal many great and important things pertainig to the Kingdom of God". All that the Lord reveals unto us comes through those He has called as Prophets. In Amos chapter three verse seven we learn that “…God reveals His secret unto His servant the prophets", there is no greater way to receive the words of the Lord except through these men.

In the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day saints, general conference are held twice every year thus in the months of April and October. During this period members of The Church around the world gather together to listen to the Voice of the Prophets as they reveal unto us what the Lord will want us do.

Below are selections from past conferences and new revelations given us by the Lord's Chosen in the most recent general Conference.

Revelation


Revelation given to the church since time past and we still receiving lots more revelation from the Lord through the Prophets. We believe we are yet to receive many great and important things in preparing for the second coming of the Savior.

Prepare to hear the Words


As General Conference week approaches, members of The Church are encourage to prepare themselves both spiritually and physically to hear the voice of the Prophets. In preparing, members are adviced to prepare by fasting and praying and also writing down questions to find answers to them during the conferece.

They seek the Lord's counsel


Each speaker during the general conferences seek the Lord's counsel on what to say to His children.
